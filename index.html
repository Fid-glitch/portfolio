I sit between code and design — and I like it there.

I'm a Computer Science student at Jyothi Engineering College who enjoys combining code and design in the same space. My work often lives at the intersection of technical problem-solving and visual communication — whether I'm designing an interface, building a Python tool, or creating posters for events.

I'm especially interested in interface design, creative websites, and projects that turn technical ideas into something people can easily understand and use. The best digital experiences are the ones where you can't tell where the engineering ends and the design begins.

When I'm not in front of a screen, I'm usually thinking about how to make the next thing I build feel a little more human.
